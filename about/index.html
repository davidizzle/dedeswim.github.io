About Me

I am a Computer Engineering student at Polytechnic of Turin, proudly Executive Board member at JEToP, the Polytechnic of Turin’s Junior Enterprise, as International Manager, responsible for public relations and partnerships, “F. Morosini” Military Naval School alumnus.

I interned at the IT Consulting firm Blue Reply, part of part of Reply Group, working on RPA and on the development of a chatbot. I spent last year as an exchange student at Tongji University in Shanghai, taking part in PoliTong project.

I am a tech enthusiast, working hard to become an AI Research Scientist, interested in research about optimization and security in/with ML. I am also swimming, sailing, and travel addicted. I like to call myself a beta tester.
